Every Cam

Every camera 4 everyone

Free online desktop recording app. Screenshare recording, webcam overlay, inverted document camera, and screen capture - all in one free app. Other apps charge subscription fees that can be prohibitive for educators and small business owners. Every Cam was developed and continues to evolve to solve these needs.


Every Cam was created and is maintained by a single developer - Neal Easterling. Please consider donating to help continue development and keep this app free 4 everyone.
