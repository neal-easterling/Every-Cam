About

Video recording while sharing a screen has become expensive. Other free versions are desktop applications that take time to learn to use. Every Cam was created to be an easy-to-use, free option for educators and small business owners. Create that tutorial to help your students. Make an online ad for your new etsy product. Use your webcam as a document camera. All you need is a webcam with internet access.

Every Cam was created and is maintained by a single developer - Neal Easterling. Please consider donating to help keep this app free for everyone.
